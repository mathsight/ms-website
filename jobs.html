At MathSight, we apply new technology and clever techniques to advise clients in their quest to perform search engine optimisation (SEO). We deal with large amounts of data that is periodically gathered, en masse, from our clientsâ€™ websites and various web-based APIs. With this data we are building a suite of processing applications that will revolutionise the way SEO is done.

These applications will deploy machine-learning techniques in order to enhance and scale up our resident (human) SEO expertise. As an intern , you will provide the team with a core programming skill set and entrepreneurial flair to help us to build a fundamental link in the chain; the leap from prototype to working, presentable and scalable application. This will enable our start-up company to tend to an increasing number of clients and with far more accuracy in the area of site optimisation.

As a technical intern your job will be of relatively high responsibility and encompass a wide-range of tasks, given that this is a small start-up. You will work with our java developer and the chief systems architect and ensure that prototype back-end analytical algorithms are transferred into Python and /or Java and will eventually feed into JavaScript, CSS and HTML as part of a slick, customer-facing web interface and API.

We would like to emphasise that this is not a straightforward developer role; rather it will involve a broader skillset with design of algorithms, creative ability and a view to create an extremely well presented product.
