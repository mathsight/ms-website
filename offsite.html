FAQs

What is an offsite page?
An offsite page is any external page, or 'backlink' that links to your own domain. We use AHREFs to gather details of these backlinks.
Why does MathSight monitor offsite pages?
It has been shown that recent Google algorithm updates have targetted the quality of offsite content, hence we saw a need to include this information in our analyses.
